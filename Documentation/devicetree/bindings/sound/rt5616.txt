RT5616 audio CODEC

This device supports I2C only.

Required properties:

- compatible : "realtek,rt5616".

- reg : The I2C address of the device.

Optional properties:

Pins on the device (for linking into audio routes) for RT5616:

  * IN1P
  * IN2P
  * IN2N
  * LOUTL
  * LOUTR
  * CPN2
  * CPP2
  * CPN1
  * CPP1
  * HPO_R
  * HPO_L
  * ADCDAT1
  * DACDAT1
  * LRCK1
  * BCLK1
  * MCLK
  * GPIO1

Example:

rt5616 {
	compatible = "realtek,rt5616";
	reg = <0x1b>;
};
